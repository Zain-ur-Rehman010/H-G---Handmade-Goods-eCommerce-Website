Who We Are

Bringing Artisan Craftsmanship to Your Home

At H&G, we believe that every handmade piece tells a story. Our mission is to connect talented artisans with people who appreciate the beauty of handcrafted goods.

Founded in 2020, H&G started as a small passion project to support local artisans during challenging times. Today, we've grown into a thriving community of over 200 skilled craftspeople from across the country, each bringing their unique traditions and techniques to create one-of-a-kind pieces.

Our Values

What Drives Us Every Day

The principles that guide everything we do

Authenticity

Every piece is genuinely handcrafted by skilled artisans. We verify each maker's process to ensure you receive authentic handmade products.

Sustainability

We prioritize eco-friendly materials and sustainable practices. Our artisans use responsibly sourced materials whenever possible.

Community

We're building a community of makers and lovers of handmade goods. Every purchase supports an artisan's livelihood and their family.

Quality

We never compromise on quality. Each item goes through careful inspection to ensure it meets our high standards before reaching you.

Our Journey

The H&G Story

How we grew from an idea to a thriving community

2020

The Beginning

H&G was born during the pandemic as a way to help local artisans sell their products online. Started with just 10 artisans.

2021

Growing Community

Expanded to 50 artisans across 15 states. Launched our artisan verification program to ensure authenticity.

2022

National Recognition

Featured in major publications. Reached 5,000 happy customers and launched our sustainability initiative.

2023

Community Impact

Partnered with craft schools to support the next generation of artisans. Launched the H&G Foundation.

2024

Today & Beyond

Over 200 artisans, 10,000+ customers, and growing. Committed to making handmade accessible to everyone.
